Should Superheroes Be Taxed?

With the rise of superheroes around the world, a new debate has emerged in political circles: should superheroes be taxed? While they provide immense value by saving cities from destruction and catching villains, they also use public infrastructure like roads, bridges, and even airspace. Not to mention, the collateral damage often resulting from their battles.

On the other hand, many argue that superheroes, with their selfless acts, already 'pay' their dues. Why should they be taxed when they are saving the world at their own personal risk? Would a tax disincentivize superhero activities? This debate rages on...
